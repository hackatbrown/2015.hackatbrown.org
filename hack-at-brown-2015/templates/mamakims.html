Dreams.

Hack@Brown is about achieving dreams. As we come into our third year, we had to ask ourselves:

What are our dreams?

And we realized something. It isn't about teaching students computer science. It's not about bringing together designers, developers and students of the humanities together in harmonious collaboration in pursuit of greater good through technology. It's about something that's fundamental to Brown CS, very near and dear to our hearts.

It's about food.

Today, we're re-aligning our organization with our true fundamental vision:

food deserves to be free

It's about Andy Van Dam's original dream for the CIT: an East Asian food restaurant, for every student, for every class.

We're acquiring Mama Kim's.

And we're building some great things with it.
